Summary of AIDS cases in the United States
With advances in medicine, technology and awareness, it would stand to reason that the spread of the AIDS virus would be on the decline. Using a small, but comprehensive dataset as part of a classroom assignment, we looked at four different demographics to see the trend in the number of AIDS cases in the United States between 2011 and 2017.
